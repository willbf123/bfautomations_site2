How It Works

Implementation Process

No surprises, no scope creep. Five clear stages from first call to live system.

  1. 01

    Call Booked

    30 min · Free

    A no-pressure video call so we can learn how your business actually runs today. You walk us through the rough shape of your week, the tools you use, and the bits that feel like a grind. By the end of the call we both know whether there's a fit.

  2. 02

    Business Audit

    Deep dive

    We map every recurring workflow, every handoff, and every tool already in your stack. We score each process on time-cost, error-rate, and automation potential. You get a written audit showing exactly where the hours are leaking and what's worth fixing first.

  3. 03

    Collaborative System Design

    Back-and-forth

    This is where the work becomes yours. We sit with you (in person or on Loom), talk through the trade-offs, and iterate on the proposed system until it genuinely fits the way you work. Nothing gets built until you nod at the design.

  4. 04

    Scope Document & Contract

    Signed, sealed

    You receive a detailed scope document that lists exactly what's included — every trigger, every integration, every deliverable. We sign a plain-English contract alongside it, so there are no discrepancies later about what was promised.

  5. 05

    Build, Launch & Retainer

    One-time fee + monthly

    We build the system, test it against real data, and hand it over. The build itself is a one-time fee. After launch, the monthly retainer kicks in — covering hosting, monitoring, tweaks, and a standing slot for new automation requests. It's part of the contract, and you can cancel it any time, no questions asked.

One-time build fee

Fixed price agreed in the scope document. Covers the full build, testing, and handover. Paid once.

Monthly retainer

Hosting, monitoring, tweaks, and a standing slot for new automation requests. Included as part of the contract.

Cancel any time

Stay because it's working — not because of a 12-month lock-in. One month's notice, no exit fees.
